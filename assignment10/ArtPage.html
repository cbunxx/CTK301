My Artwork

My Process!

Though I also do multimedia traditional art, the majority of my best work is done digitally through the Procreate app on my iPad Air 4. I work with a mixture of textured pencil brushes and smoothe ink brushes, gaussian blur, paper/grain overlay, and a warm green-tinted color palette. I am open to trying new styles and being flexible with commission requests!
