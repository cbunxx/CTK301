My Process!

Though I also do multimedia traditional art, the majority of my best work is done digitally through the Procreate app on my iPad Air 4. I usually work with a variation of HB and 6B pencil brushes, syrup ink brushes, gaussian blur, and a warm green-tinted color palette. I am open to experimenting with new styles and being flexible with commission requests!
